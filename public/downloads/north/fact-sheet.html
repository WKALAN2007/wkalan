Company Overview

North is a memory technology company based in Portland, Oregon. We build living archives — tools that help families preserve their stories across generations. Unlike cloud storage, which treats your memories as files, North treats them as stories: connected, alive, and built to last.

Technology

On-device AI: Computer vision and audio processing run locally. Your data never leaves your device unencrypted.

Knowledge Graph: Proprietary relationship mapping connects every memory — by person, place, time, and context.

Century Storage: Open, documented file formats with embedded recovery metadata. Designed to be readable in 100 years.

Geographic Redundancy: Encrypted data distributed across multiple regions. No single point of failure.

Leadership

David Kim — Founder & CEO. Previously cloud infrastructure engineer. Started North after his father's Alzheimer's diagnosis.

Sarah Okafor — Co-founder & Design. Eight years designing storage interfaces. Believes memory tools should feel like a family album, not a spreadsheet.
