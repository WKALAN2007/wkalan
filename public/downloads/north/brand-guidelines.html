North Brand Guidelines

Last updated: January 2025

Logo

The North wordmark is set in Instrument Serif. Always use the provided logo files. Minimum clear space is equal to the height of the "N".

Colors

Primary: #0F0F0D
Surface: #1A1A18
Accent: #C9A84C

Typography

Headings: Instrument Serif
Body: Geist Sans
Mono: Geist Mono

Brand Voice

Thoughtful. Honest. Human. We write like we speak — never corporate, never hype. Every word should feel like it was written by someone who cares.
