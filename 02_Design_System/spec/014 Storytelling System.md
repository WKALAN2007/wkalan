# 014 — Storytelling System

## Philosophy

**Every page is a story. Every story has a rhythm.**

WKALAN doesn't design pages. We design narratives. A visitor doesn't "browse" a WKALAN site — they experience it. They stop. They think. They feel. They believe. They act.

This is not a marketing tactic. It's the natural consequence of 品味人生. When you truly understand someone, you can tell their story. When you tell their story, others feel it.

---

## Principles

### Every story follows the same arc.
Curiosity → Understanding → Connection → Trust → Action. Not "Home → About → Portfolio → Contact." The emotional journey, not the information architecture.

### Show. Don't tell.
Don't say "we understand creators." Show a creator's story. Don't say "we care about craft." Show the interview notes. Don't say "our process is different." Walk them through it.

### Every section answers one question.
If a section doesn't answer a specific question in the reader's mind, it doesn't belong. Sections are not filler. They're answers.

### The story is about the person. Not about us.
Even on WKALAN's own site, the story is about the visitor's problem (being reduced to numbers) and the people we've helped. WKALAN is the narrator, not the protagonist.

---

## Specification

### The Narrative Arc

| Chapter | Question | Emotion | Action |
|---------|----------|---------|--------|
| **Stop** | "What is this place?" | Curiosity | Keep reading |
| **Problem** | "Why does this matter?" | Recognition | Nodding |
| **Origin** | "Where did this come from?" | Connection | Leaning in |
| **Philosophy** | "How do they think?" | Trust | Believing |
| **Process** | "How does it work?" | Clarity | Understanding |
| **Proof** | "Does it actually work?" | Belief | Wanting it |
| **Invitation** | "How do I start?" | Decision | Acting |

### Section Rhythm

```
Full-bleed (emotional impact)
  ↓
Contained (intellectual processing)
  ↓
Dark (intimacy, personal revelation)
  ↓
Light (clarity, methodology)
  ↓
Surface (proof, evidence)
  ↓
Dark (decision, action)
```

### Pacing Rules
- **Open with feeling.** Chapter 1 is emotional, not informational.
- **The turning point is Chapters 2–3.** This is where they go from "interesting" to "I want this."
- **End with clarity.** The final chapter must answer: "What do I do next?"

---

## Examples

### ✅ Correct
> WKALAN homepage. Chapter 1: cinematic photo + "品味人生。" (STOP). Chapter 2: "但，真正的人呢？" (THINK). Chapter 3: Camino story (CONNECT). Chapters 4-5: Philosophy + Process (TRUST). Chapter 6: Stories (BELIEVE). Chapter 7: "Let's tell your story." (ACT).
>
> **Why:** The emotional arc is clear. Each section answers one question. The visitor never wonders "why is this here?"

### ❌ Incorrect
> Agency homepage: Hero (with CTA) → Services (list) → Portfolio (grid) → Testimonials (carousel) → Contact (form).
>
> **Why:** Information without emotion. No arc. No rhythm. The visitor gets the facts but feels nothing. They leave without remembering.
