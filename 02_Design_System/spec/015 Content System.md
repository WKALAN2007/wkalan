# 015 — Content System

## Philosophy

**Every word earns its place — or it's deleted.**

WKALAN content is not filler. It's not SEO fodder. It's not "lorem ipsum with keywords." Every sentence exists to move the reader through the emotional journey: Stop → Think → Resonate → Believe → Act.

---

## Principles

### Brevity is respect.
Say it in five words. Not ten. The reader's attention is a gift. Honor it.

### Honesty over hype.
No adjectives that can't be proven. "Beautiful" is an opinion. "She's been making videos since 2019" is a fact.

### One idea per paragraph.
Dense paragraphs are walls. Short paragraphs are invitations. Give every idea its own space.

### Chinese leads emotion. English leads clarity.
WKALAN content flows in both languages. Chinese carries warmth and poetry. English carries precision and reach.

---

## Specification

### Content Architecture
| Section | Primary Language | Role |
|---------|-----------------|------|
| Hero statement | Chinese | Emotional hook |
| Subtitle / explainer | English | Clarity for global audience |
| Labels | English | Universal recognition |
| Body text | Chinese or English | Depends on client and audience |
| CTAs | Chinese | Warmth in invitation |

### Copy Patterns
- **Headlines:** Short. Serif. Chinese for emotion. "品味人生。" Not "We design digital identities for creators."
- **Labels:** One word. Uppercase. Wide tracking. "Chapter 1." "The Problem."
- **Body:** 18px. 1.6 line height. Max 680px width. One idea per paragraph.
- **CTAs:** Invitations, not commands. "开始对话" (Start a conversation). Not "Contact Us."

### What We Never Write
- "Welcome to our website"
- "We are passionate about..."
- "Leveraging cutting-edge technology"
- "Synergy," "ecosystem," "holistic," "innovative"
- Any sentence ChatGPT would write unprompted

---

## Examples

### ✅ Correct
> "17岁那年，我一个人走完了圣地亚哥朝圣之路。800公里。在那条路上，我遇到来自世界各地的陌生人。每一个人，都带着自己的故事。"

**Why:** Specific. Personal. Concrete details (17岁, 800公里, 圣地亚哥). The reader sees it.

### ❌ Incorrect
> "Our founder embarked on a transformative journey of self-discovery, walking over 800 kilometers across Spain, where he gained profound insights into the human condition."

**Why:** Generic. Inflated. Reads like a press release. The reader feels nothing.
