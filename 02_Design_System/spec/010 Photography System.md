# 010 — Photography System

## Philosophy

**A photograph says: "This person exists. This really happened."**

WKALAN photography is documentary, not advertising. Natural light. Real moments. The person's actual space. Imperfection is character — it's proof a human took this photograph.

---

## Principles

### Natural light. No studio.
Light from a window. Light from the sun. Light that existed in the moment. Never ring lights. Never studio flash.

### Unposed when possible.
The best photographs happen when someone forgot the camera was there. Laughing. Thinking. Looking away.

### Context matters.
Shoot people in their space. Their desk. Their kitchen. Their studio. The environment tells as much story as the face.

### Never stock. Never AI.
WKALAN rejects stock photography and AI-generated imagery entirely. Every photograph must be of the actual person.

---

## Specification

| Context | Ratio | Style |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Hero portrait | 16:9 or 3:4 | Natural light, not looking at camera |
| Work / process | 3:4 or 1:1 | In their space, candid |
| Gallery | 3:4 | Consistent ratio within gallery |
| Avatar | 1:1 | Warm, approachable |

### Image Treatment
Full-bleed (no borders, no shadows). Dark gradient overlay when text overlays images. Subtle warmth (-5% saturation on editorial style). Black and white only when color doesn't serve the story.

---

## Examples

### ✅ Correct
> Founder portrait. Natural light from a window. Not looking at the camera. Sitting at their actual desk. The room tells you about them.

### ❌ Incorrect
> AI-generated portrait of "a creative professional in a modern office." Perfect lighting. Perfect composition. Zero truth.
