# 024 — Quality Checklist

## Philosophy

**Good design is never random.**

Every WKALAN project, before it ships, must answer these questions. Not as a formality. As a genuine test of whether the work reflects the philosophy.

---

## The WKALAN Quality Checklist

### 品味 — Savor

- [ ] **Did we listen before we designed?**
  Did we spend hours in conversation before opening Figma? Did we ask questions that let the person reveal themselves?

- [ ] **Does the design come from the person's story?**
  Can we trace every major design decision back to something the client said, showed us, or made us feel?

- [ ] **Did we capture the ordinary, not just the dramatic?**
  Is there space for the everyday? The morning routine? The small detail that only they would mention?

### 雕刻 — Carve

- [ ] **Did we remove everything that doesn't belong?**
  Is there anything on this page that's here because WE thought it looked good — not because it's THEM?

- [ ] **Is every color, typeface, and animation justified?**
  Can we explain why each one is there? "It looked good" is not an explanation.

- [ ] **Does the design have breathing room?**
  Is there enough whitespace for the content to be savored? Or is everything crowded, competing, urgent?

### 呈现 — Reveal

- [ ] **Does it feel like meeting a person?**
  If a stranger visits this site, would they feel like they know the person afterward?

- [ ] **Is the person visible, or is the design visible?**
  If someone describes the site, do they describe the person — or the design?

- [ ] **Does it feel true?**
  Not impressive. Not professional. Not on-trend. TRUE. Does the person recognize themselves in it?

### Craft

- [ ] **Is every easing curve consistent?**
  One curve. One duration scale. No random animations.

- [ ] **Is the spacing consistent throughout?**
  Every section follows the spacing scale. No one-off padding values.

- [ ] **Is the type scale applied correctly?**
  Headlines, body, captions all use the defined sizes. No custom font-size declarations.

- [ ] **Does it pass accessibility standards?**
  Contrast. Focus states. Alt text. Keyboard navigation. Screen reader compatible.

- [ ] **Does it work on mobile?**
  Every section, every image, every interaction — tested on a phone.

- [ ] **Is the performance acceptable?**
  Images are optimized. Fonts are loaded. No layout shift. No jank.

### Soul

- [ ] **Does this project make us proud?**
  Would we put our name on it? Would we show it to a stranger and say "we made this"?

- [ ] **Did we honor the person's trust?**
  They shared their story with us. Did we treat it with the care it deserves?

- [ ] **Would we do anything differently if we had more time?**
  If yes — what? And why didn't we do it?

---

## The Final Test

After every checkbox is checked, there's one more question:

**Does someone leave this site feeling like they met a person?**

If yes — ship it.

If no — go back. Not to fix the design. To understand the person better.

---

*Quality is not the absence of mistakes. Quality is the presence of care.*
