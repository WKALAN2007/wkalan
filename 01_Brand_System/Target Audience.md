# Target Audience

## Primary Audience: The Unseen Creator

### Who They Are
Creators who have spent years building audiences — producing content, growing followings, mastering their platforms — but feel increasingly that **nobody actually knows them.**

### Demographics
- **Age:** 20–35
- **Platform:** Bilibili, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram
- **Following:** 10K–500K+
- **Content type:** Video, writing, art, music, education
- **Location:** China, global Chinese diaspora, English-speaking markets
- **Stage:** Mid-career — established but seeking depth

### Psychographics
- Frustrated by being reduced to metrics
- Feels their content doesn't reflect their full self
- Values craft and authenticity over trends
- Has a story worth telling, but no space to tell it
- Willing to invest in something lasting
- Tired of templated solutions

### Their Inner Monologue
*"I have 300,000 followers. But does anyone actually know who I am?"*

*"I make content about basketball. But I'm also a son, a dreamer, a person with fears and hopes and stories that don't fit in a 3-minute video."*

*"My Bilibili page has my videos. My WeChat has my contacts. But where is ME?"*

## Secondary Audience: The Story-Rich Founder

### Who They Are
Entrepreneurs, indie makers, and startup founders whose personal story is their greatest asset — but whose online presence doesn't reflect their depth.

### Demographics
- **Age:** 25–45
- **Role:** Founder, solopreneur, indie maker
- **Stage:** Early-stage to growth
- **Need:** A digital identity that attracts investors, partners, customers — by revealing the person behind the product

## Tertiary Audience: The Depth-Seeking Brand

### Who They Are
Small brands, studios, and collectives that want to communicate with the intimacy of a person, not the polish of a corporation.

### Demographics
- **Type:** Fashion brands, design studios, creative collectives
- **Size:** 1–10 people
- **Need:** A digital presence that feels personal and editorial, not commercial

## Who We Don't Serve

- **Enterprises** — We're not an agency. Scale isn't our game.
- **Trend-chasers** — If you want what's hot right now, we're not for you.
- **Metric-obsessed** — If conversion rate is your only KPI, find a growth hacker.
- **Rushed** — If you need it in two weeks, we can't help you.
