# 003 — Service Architecture

## The Three Tiers

WKALAN offers three levels of digital identity. Each tier has a clear scope, timeline, and price. No custom quotes. No negotiation. Pick the tier. Trust the process.

## Service Rules

### 1. No custom quotes
Three tiers. Pick one. If you need something between tiers, pick the higher one.

### 2. Timeline is fixed
5, 10, or 14 days. Not negotiable. Depth takes the time it takes. Rushing produces templates.

### 3. Payment: 50% upfront, 50% before launch
No deposit = no start. No final payment = no launch. Simple. Professional.

### 4. No scope creep
The tier defines the scope. Additional requests go into a "Phase 2" backlog — quoted separately.

### 5. Discovery is non-negotiable
Even at Sample tier, the 1-hour interview is mandatory. No interview = no understanding = no WKALAN.
