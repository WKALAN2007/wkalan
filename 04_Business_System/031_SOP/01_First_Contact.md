# SOP 01 — First Contact

## When someone reaches out for the first time.

### Goal
Respond within 24 hours. Qualify the lead. Book a discovery call.

### Process

**Step 1: Read their message.**
What did they say? What platform? What tier are they asking about? Did they mention a specific case study or creator?

**Step 2: Respond with warmth.**
Template:
> Hi [Name] — thanks for reaching out. WKALAN is a digital identity studio. We build spaces where creators can actually be known — not just followed.
>
> Our process always starts with a free 30-minute call. No pitch. Just a conversation to understand what you're looking for — and whether we're a good fit.
>
> Here's a link to book a time: [Calendly link]
>
> Talk soon,
> Alan

**Step 3: Track.**
Log in your CRM (Notion/Google Sheet):
- Name
- Platform
- Date of first contact
- Status: Awaiting call booking

**Step 4: Follow up (if no booking after 3 days).**
> Hi [Name] — just checking in. No rush at all. Just wanted to make sure you saw the booking link. If the timing doesn't work, let me know and we'll figure something out.

### Quality Gate
- [ ] Response within 24 hours
- [ ] Warm, human tone — no template-speak
- [ ] Calendly link included
- [ ] Logged in CRM
