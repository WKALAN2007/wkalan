# SOP 02 — Discovery Call

## The free 30-minute conversation. Not a sales call.

### Goal
Understand what they need. Qualify the fit. End with a clear next step.

### Process

**Step 1: Prepare (5 min before).**
- Review their platform/content if they shared it.
- Open their CRM entry.
- Have the Service Architecture open for reference.

**Step 2: The Call Structure (30 min).**

*Minute 0–5: Warm open.*
"Thanks for jumping on. Before we talk about websites — tell me about what you do. What kind of creator are you?"

*Minute 5–15: Listen.*
Let them talk about their work. Their audience. Their frustration with their current online presence.
Ask: "What's missing between your content and how people see you online?"

*Minute 15–25: Share.*
Explain WKALAN's approach. Not a pitch. A description.
"We don't start with design. We start with understanding — hours of conversation before any pixel is placed. The goal is that someone who visits your site leaves feeling like they met you."

*Minute 25–30: Next step.*
If it's a fit: "I think we could do something special. Here's how it works: [explain tiers]. Which feels right to you?"
If not a fit: "I don't think WKALAN is the right match right now. Here's what I'd recommend instead: [honest advice]."

**Step 3: Follow up (within 2 hours).**
> Great talking with you today. Here's a quick recap:
> - You're looking for: [summary]
> - Recommended tier: [Sample / Advance / Pro]
> - Next step: [confirm booking / send proposal / etc.]
>
> Talk soon.

### Quality Gate
- [ ] They talked more than you did (60%+ them)
- [ ] You asked at least 3 questions from the Interview Framework
- [ ] Clear next step agreed
- [ ] Follow-up sent within 2 hours
- [ ] CRM updated
