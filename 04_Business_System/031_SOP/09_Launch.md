# SOP 09 — Launch

## The identity goes live.

### Goal
Flawless deployment. Client celebrates. WKALAN documents.

### Process

**Step 1: Pre-Launch Checklist.**
- [ ] `024 Quality Checklist.md` — all items passed
- [ ] `029 Engineering Checklist.md` — all items passed
- [ ] Mobile tested (iPhone SE, iPhone 14, iPad)
- [ ] Desktop tested (1280px, 1440px)
- [ ] All links working (no 404s)
- [ ] All images loading (no broken images)
- [ ] Meta tags verified (OG image, title, description)
- [ ] Custom domain configured (if applicable)
- [ ] Client has reviewed and approved

**Step 2: Deploy.**
- Push to production (Vercel)
- Verify deployment (visit live URL)
- Wait 5 minutes. Check again.

**Step 3: Client Handoff.**
> Hi [Name] — it's live.
>
> [URL]
>
> Take a moment. Open it. This is you, online, for the world to meet.
>
> When you're ready, here's a suggested post for your audience:
> [suggested caption + 3 screenshots]
>
> I'll check in next week to see how it's feeling. If anything needs adjusting before then, just message me.
>
> Congratulations. You're no longer just a creator with content — you're a person with a digital identity.
>
> Alan

**Step 4: Document.**
- Update CRM: Status → "Launched"
- Save screenshots for case study
- Schedule 1-week check-in reminder
- Schedule 1-month check-in reminder

**Step 5: Celebrate.**
Seriously. You just helped someone be known. That's worth a moment.

### Quality Gate
- [ ] All checklists passed
- [ ] Live URL working
- [ ] Client handoff message sent
- [ ] Screenshots saved
- [ ] CRM updated
- [ ] Check-in reminders scheduled
