# SOP 04 — Onboarding

## From "Yes" to "Let's Begin."

### Goal
Deposit received. Interview booked. Client feels excited and safe.

### Process

**Step 1: Receive deposit.**
50% upfront. Before any work begins.
Payment method: [WeChat Pay / Alipay / bank transfer]

**Step 2: Send Welcome Pack.**
> Hi [Name] — deposit received. Welcome to WKALAN.
>
> Here's what happens next:
> 1. **Interview** — We'll spend [1/2/3] hour(s) together. This is the most important part. I'll ask you questions nobody's asked you before — about your life, your work, who you really are.
> 2. **Design Direction** — I'll translate what I heard into a visual direction. You'll see it before any code is written.
> 3. **Build** — I'll build your digital identity. You'll get daily updates.
> 4. **Launch** — We go live. You announce it to your audience.
>
> First step: book your interview here: [Calendly link]
>
> A few things to know:
> - No need to prepare anything. Just show up as yourself.
> - The interview will be recorded (with your permission). It helps me capture everything.
> - You can ask me anything at any point. I'm here.
>
> Talk soon,
> Alan

**Step 3: Create project folder.**
- CRM: mark as "Active — Onboarding"
- Create project timeline (based on tier)
- Schedule internal reminders for each milestone

### Quality Gate
- [ ] Deposit received
- [ ] Welcome Pack sent
- [ ] Interview booked on calendar
- [ ] Project folder created
- [ ] Client responded with excitement, not anxiety
