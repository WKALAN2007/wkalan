# SOP 03 — Proposal

## Converting a discovery call into a commitment.

### Goal
Send a clear, confident proposal. No pitching. No negotiating. Just: "Here's what we'll do."

### Process

**Step 1: Prepare the proposal.**
Use the template in `032_Templates/Proposal.md`.

Fill in:
- Client name
- Recommended tier
- Timeline
- Key points from the discovery call (show you were listening)

**Step 2: Send within 24 hours of the call.**

Template:
> Hi [Name] — here's the proposal we discussed.
>
> Tier: [Sample / Advance / Pro]
> Timeline: [X] days
> Investment: HK$[amount]
>
> No rush to decide. If it feels right, the next step is a 50% deposit to lock in the start date. If you have questions, I'm here.
>
> Alan

**Step 3: Follow up (if no response after 5 days).**
> Hi [Name] — just checking in. No pressure at all. If the timing isn't right or if you have questions, happy to chat.

**Step 4: After that, let it go.**
Don't chase more than once. If they're not ready, they're not ready. The right clients come back.

### Quality Gate
- [ ] Proposal sent within 24 hours
- [ ] Clear tier, timeline, price
- [ ] References something specific from the discovery call
- [ ] No discounting. No negotiating.
