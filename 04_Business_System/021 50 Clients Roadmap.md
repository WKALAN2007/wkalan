## The Strategy

### Phase 1: Months 1–3 — Prove (10 clients)
**Goal:** Get the first 10 projects done. Build the case studies. Prove the process works.

**Actions:**
- DM 50 Bilibili creators who don't have personal sites
- Offer "founding client" rate: 20% off any tier
- Focus on Sample and Advance — faster turnaround, more volume
- Document everything. Every interview. Every design decision.
- By end of Phase 1: 3 case studies published

**Metric:** 10 shipped projects. 3 case studies. 2 referrals.

### Phase 2: Months 4–6 — Grow (15 clients)
**Goal:** Increase volume. Raise prices. Build reputation.

**Actions:**
- Founding client rate ends. Full price now.
- Push for Pro tier — case studies are the proof
- Ask every client for a referral
- Post case study content on Xiaohongshu / Bilibili
- Start building email list of potential clients

**Metric:** 15 shipped projects. 5 case studies. 5 referrals.

### Phase 3: Months 7–9 — Scale (15 clients)
**Goal:** Smooth operations. Trainable process. First team thinking.

**Actions:**
- Process is repeatable now. Document every variation.
- Start thinking about hiring (designer #1, end of Year 1)
- Template components in 03_Engineering_System
- Batch similar-tier projects together for efficiency
- Explore partnerships: Bilibili creator agencies, MCNs

**Metric:** 15 shipped projects. Process documentation complete. Hiring plan drafted.

### Phase 4: Months 10–12 — Sustain (10 clients)
**Goal:** Maintain momentum. Plan Year 2. Prepare for 100.

**Actions:**
- Holiday slowdown expected. Fill pipeline early.
- Review year: what worked? what broke? what needs to change?
- Plan Year 2 pricing increases
- Draft Year 2 strategy: 100 clients, first hire, CMS development
- Celebrate. You built a business.

**Metric:** 10 shipped projects. Year 2 strategy document. 50 total for the year.

## What Makes This Possible

### 1. Fixed-price tiers
No scoping. No proposals. No negotiation. Client picks a tier. Process starts. This saves days of admin per client.

### 2. Documented process
Every step is in the Playbook. No reinventing. No forgetting. No "how did we do this last time?"

### 3. Design system leverage
`02_Design_System/` means every project starts from a foundation, not from scratch. Sample tier especially — 80% system, 20% customization.

### 4. AI acceleration
AI transcribes interviews. AI extracts keywords. AI generates first drafts. Human reviews everything. Time saved: 30–40%.

### 5. Referral engine
Every client who feels understood refers at least one person. By client #10, referrals become the primary acquisition channel.

## Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Burnout | Fixed timelines. No overwork. Process protects you. |
| Quality drop at volume | Design system ensures baseline. Templates prevent reinvention. |
| Client delay (content, feedback) | Clear deadlines in contract. Delay = project pauses, not your problem. |
| Price pushback | Case studies justify the price. Don't negotiate — refer to Advance or Sample. |
| Cash flow gaps | 50% upfront for every project. Always have a buffer. |
