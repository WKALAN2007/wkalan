# 002 — Ideal Client Profile

## Who We Serve

WKALAN exists for one person:

**The creator who has an audience but has never been truly known.**

---

## The WKALAN Ideal Client

### Demographics
| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age** | 20–35 |
| **Location** | China (primary), Chinese diaspora, expanding to global |
| **Platform** | Bilibili, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram |
| **Following** | 10K–500K+ |
| **Content type** | Video, writing, art, education, entertainment |
| **Income** | Making money from content — but not rich. Investing in identity is a stretch worth making. |

### Psychographics
| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Frustration** | "I have followers. But does anyone actually know me?" |
| **Desire** | Wants to be seen as more than their content niche |
| **Values** | Authenticity, craft, depth, longevity |
| **Fear** | Being forgotten. Being reduced to numbers. Being replaced by AI. |
| **Aspiration** | Wants their online presence to feel like *them* — not like content optimization |

### Behavior
| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Research style** | Asks friends. Looks at other creators' sites. Searches Bilibili/Reddit. |
| **Decision driver** | "This person actually gets me" — not price, not portfolio |
| **Budget mindset** | Willing to invest if they feel understood. Skeptical of "cheap and fast." |
| **Referral likelihood** | High — if they feel understood, they tell everyone |

## Who Is NOT Our Client

| Profile | Why Not |
|---------|--------|
| **Enterprise executives** | They need LinkedIn optimization, not digital identity. Wrong channel. |
| **Budget-only shoppers** | "What's your cheapest option?" — They want a website, not understanding. |
| **Rush clients** | "I need it in 3 days." — Depth takes time. We don't rush. |
| **Template-happy** | "Can you just use a template and make it look nice?" — No. |
| **Ghost clients** | Won't show up for discovery calls. Won't share their story. Can't be understood. |
