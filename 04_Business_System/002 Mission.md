# 002 — Mission

## What WKALAN Does Every Day

**品味人生，雕刻身份。**

Every creator who comes to WKALAN leaves with more than a website. They leave feeling understood — some of them for the first time in their online lives.

---

## The Daily Mission

| Action | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| **Listen** | One creator at a time. Hours of conversation. Real presence. |
| **Understand** | Extract what's true. Find the words they didn't know they were saying. |
| **Carve** | Remove everything that isn't them. Keep only what belongs. |
| **Build** | Translate understanding into code, type, color, motion. |
| **Ship** | Deliver an identity. Not a template. Not a portfolio. An identity. |
| **Document** | Every project feeds the system. Every case study proves the method. |

---

## What We Don't Do

- Rush.
- Template.
- Guess.
- Impress.
- Decorate.
- Perform.

---

## The Only Metric That Matters

**Did someone leave this site feeling like they met a person?**

Everything else — revenue, growth, headcount — is secondary. This is the metric. This is the mission.

---

*Mission is not what you say you do. It's what you refuse to compromise on.*
