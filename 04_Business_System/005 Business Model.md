# 005 — Business Model

## How WKALAN Creates and Captures Value

WKALAN is a **service-first, product-second** business. We start with high-touch custom work. Over time, we productize the methodology into tools, education, and platforms.

---

## Phase 1: Studio (Year 1–2)
**Revenue model:** Project-based services

| Tier | Price | Timeline | Annual Volume (Year 1) |
|------|-------|----------|------------------------|
| Sample | HK$500 | 5 days | 25 |
| Advance | HK$1,000 | 10 days | 15 |
| Pro | HK$2,000 | 14 days | 10 |

**Key metrics:**
- 50 clients/year
- ~HK$47,500 revenue
- 100% founder-delivered
- Case studies as primary marketing

---

## Phase 2: Studio + Products (Year 3–4)
**Revenue model:** Services + digital products + education

| Stream | Description | Annual Revenue Target |
|--------|-------------|----------------------|
| WKALAN Studio | Custom projects (now at higher prices) | HK$100,000 |
| WKALAN OS License | Design system + components for other designers | HK$30,000 |
| AI Prompt Library | Curated prompts for brand, design, code | HK$15,000 |
| WKALAN Academy | Online courses teaching the methodology | HK$50,000 |

**Key metrics:**
- 100+ clients/year (services)
- First hire (operations)
- Product revenue > 30% of total

---

## Phase 3: Platform (Year 5+)
**Revenue model:** SaaS + services + education

| Stream | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| WKALAN Platform | Guided digital identity creation (AI-assisted self-serve) |
| WKALAN Studio | Premium human-led service (fewer clients, higher price) |
| WKALAN OS | Licensed to agencies and design teams |
| WKALAN Academy | Certifications, workshops, community |

**Key metrics:**
- 500+ identities created/year (platform + studio)
- Platform revenue > 50% of total
- Team of 5–10

---

## Business Model Principles

### 1. Services fund products
Custom work generates cash now. That cash funds the product development that scales later.

### 2. Products don't replace services
Even at scale, WKALAN Studio remains — for the clients who want the deepest experience. Products expand reach. Services maintain depth.

### 3. Fixed pricing, not hourly
Value-based pricing, not time-based. The client pays for the outcome (being understood), not the hours.

### 4. Low overhead, high margin
No office. No full-time staff (until needed). Tools cost near zero (Next.js, Vercel, open source). The main cost is time — and the value created exceeds the time invested.
