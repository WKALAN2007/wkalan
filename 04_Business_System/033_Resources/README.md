## What We Look For

When studying a company, ask:
1. **How do they position themselves?** — Not what they sell. How they're perceived.
2. **How do they talk about their work?** — Brand voice. Content strategy.
3. **How do they price?** — Value capture. Tier strategy.
4. **How do they grow?** — Marketing. Community. Referral.
5. **What would WKALAN do differently?** — Adapt, don't copy.
