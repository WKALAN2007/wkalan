# 001 — Vision

## Where WKALAN Is Going

**To become the standard for how people introduce themselves online.**

Not a website builder. Not a personal branding agency. Not a link-in-bio tool.

The standard.

When someone wants to be known online — not just watched, not just followed, not just measured — they think of WKALAN.

---

## The 10-Year Picture

### Year 1–2: Prove
- 50 clients in Year 1. 100 in Year 2.
- 10+ published case studies.
- Process documented. Playbook complete.
- Reputation: "The people who actually understand you."

### Year 3–4: Scale
- First hires: operations, then design, then engineering.
- WKALAN CMS: a lightweight system for creators to manage their digital identity.
- Design system licensed to other designers.
- AI prompt library sold as a product.

### Year 5–7: Platform
- WKALAN Platform: guided digital identity creation.
- Human understanding + AI efficiency.
- Creators can start with AI-assisted self-serve or book human-led deep identity.
- 500+ clients/year.

### Year 8–10: Standard
- "WKALAN identity" is a recognized category.
- The methodology is taught in design schools.
- The platform powers identities for creators globally.
- Alan doesn't design every site anymore. But every site still feels like someone savored the life behind it.

---

## What Success Looks Like

**Not:** A big agency with 100 employees and glass offices.

**But:** A creator somewhere opens their WKALAN site. Someone they've never met visits it. And afterward, that person says:

*"I feel like I know you now."*

That's the metric. That's the vision.
