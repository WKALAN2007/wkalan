## Brand Decisions

### We position as premium, not cheap.
"WKALAN is not for everyone" is a feature, not a bug.

### We market through work, not ads.
Every case study is marketing. Every client referral is marketing. The work speaks.

### We build for creators, not enterprises.
Niche is strength. "The digital identity studio for creators" is clearer than "we build websites for anyone."

### We lead with philosophy, not portfolio.
Most designers show their work first. We show what we believe first. Clients who resonate with the philosophy become ideal clients.
