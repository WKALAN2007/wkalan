## Cash Flow Management

### 50% Upfront Policy
Every project: 50% paid before work begins. 50% before launch.

This means:
- WKALAN always has cash on hand
- No chasing invoices after delivery
- No working for free

### Emergency Buffer
Maintain minimum HK$5,000 in the business account at all times. This covers 3 months of expenses (which are near zero).

## Financial Principles

### 1. Near-zero costs
WKALAN's stack is almost free (Next.js, Vercel Hobby, open-source tools). Maintain this. Don't add SaaS subscriptions "just in case."

### 2. No debt
Don't borrow. Don't take investment until the model is proven and the money would accelerate — not enable.

### 3. Personal salary from day one
Even if it's small. Even if it's symbolic. Pay yourself. This is a business, not a hobby. HK$1,000/month minimum by Q2.

### 4. Reinvest in growth
30% of profit goes back into WKALAN: better equipment, paid tools, contractor help, education.

### 5. Track everything
Revenue per client. Time per project. Referral source. Profit per tier. What isn't measured can't be improved.

---

*Financial plan is not about being rich. It's about being sustainable. A business that can't pay for itself can't help anyone.*
