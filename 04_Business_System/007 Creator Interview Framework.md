# 007 — Creator Interview Framework

## Philosophy

**The questions you ask determine the depth you reach.**

AI can ask "What's your favorite color?" It cannot sit with someone for an hour and notice the way their voice changes when they talk about their father.

This framework is WKALAN's moat. It's what separates us from templates, from AI generators, from every other "web designer."

---

## Interview Rules

### 1. Record everything.
Ask permission. Press record. Transcribe later. The transcript is where you find the keywords the client didn't know they were saying.

### 2. Don't interview. Converse.
A questionnaire gets answers. A conversation gets stories. Follow the tangents. Ask "tell me more about that." The best insights come from the detours.

### 3. Silence is a tool.
When they pause, wait. Don't fill the silence with the next question. Let them sit with the thought. The next thing they say is often the real answer.

### 4. Notice the voice.
When does their tone change? What topics make them speed up? Slow down? Get quiet? The emotional data is as important as the words.

### 5. One question at a time.
Never stack questions. "What shaped you growing up, and also what's your favorite movie?" — they'll answer the easy one and forget the hard one.

## The Question Bank

### Layer 1: Roots (Where you come from)

*These questions reveal the foundation. Ask slowly. Let them take their time.*

1. 你成長在一個怎樣的環境裡？
   *(What kind of environment did you grow up in?)*

2. 小時候的你，是一個怎樣的人？
   *(What kind of person were you as a child?)*

3. 你的家人對你最大的影響是什麼？
   *(What's the biggest way your family shaped you?)*

4. 有沒有某個時刻，徹底改變了你對人生的看法？
   *(Was there a moment that completely changed how you see life?)*

5. 你17歲的時候，在想什麼？
   *(When you were 17, what were you thinking about?)*

### Layer 2: Craft (What you do and why)

*These questions reveal the meaning behind the work.*

6. 為什麼你會開始做這件事（拍片、寫作、創作）？
   *(Why did you start doing this — filming, writing, creating?)*

7. 你還記得你做的第一個作品嗎？當時的感受是什麼？
   *(Do you remember your first piece of work? How did it feel?)*

8. 哪一個作品讓你最驕傲？不是播放量最高的，是你最喜歡的。
   *(Which piece are you proudest of? Not the most viewed — the one you love most.)*

9. 你有沒有做過一個作品，做完之後覺得「這才是我」？
   *(Have you ever made something and thought: "This is me"?)*

10. 創作對你來說，到底是什麼？
    *(What does creating actually mean to you?)*

### Layer 3: Identity (Who you are beyond the content)

*These questions reveal the person behind the creator.*

11. 如果你的內容明天全部消失了，你還希望留下什麼？
    *(If all your content disappeared tomorrow, what would you still want to leave behind?)*

12. 別人對你最大的誤解是什麼？
    *(What's the biggest thing people misunderstand about you?)*

13. 你有沒有一面，是觀眾從來沒見過的？
    *(Is there a side of you that your audience has never seen?)*

14. 你最好的朋友會怎麼形容你？
    *(How would your best friend describe you?)*

15. 什麼事情會讓你哭？
    *(What makes you cry?)*

### Layer 4: Legacy (How you want to be remembered)

*These questions reveal the deeper purpose.*

16. 你希望別人認識你之後，記住你什麼？
    *(When someone gets to know you, what do you want them to remember?)*

17. 如果你的網站只能留下一句話，你希望是什麼？
    *(If your website could only leave one sentence, what would it be?)*

18. 十年後，你希望別人怎麼描述你這個人？
    *(Ten years from now, how do you want people to describe you?)*

19. 如果有個完全不認識你的人走進你的網站，你希望他離開後有什麼感覺？
    *(If a complete stranger visits your site, what do you want them to feel when they leave?)*

20. 你覺得「被了解」是什麼感覺？
    *(What does it feel like — to be understood?)*

## After the Interview

### Step 1: Transcribe
Use Whisper or similar. Get the full text.

### Step 2: Extract Keywords
Read the transcript. Highlight:
- **Words they repeat** — these are anchors
- **Emotional peaks** — where their voice changed
- **Surprising answers** — things you didn't expect
- **Simple truths** — the obvious thing they think isn't worth mentioning (it is)

### Step 3: Find the 3 Words
Boil everything down to three words that are *uniquely theirs.* Not generic. Not flattering. True.

Example:
- 小白剪了個球 → "情绪 / 台词 / 不妥协"
- Hypothetical fashion creator → "布料 / 沉默 / 坚持"

### Step 4: Build from the 3 Words
Color palette. Typography. Layout rhythm. Animation style. Every design decision traces back to one of the three words.

## The Interviewer's Mindset

You are not extracting information. You are savoring a life.

品味人生.

Let them talk. Let them pause. Let them surprise themselves with what comes out.

The best moment in any WKALAN interview is when the client says:

*"Huh. I've never said that out loud before."*

That's when you know you're doing it right.

*Questions are the tool. Listening is the craft. Understanding is the product.*
