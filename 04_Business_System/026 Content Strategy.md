# 026 — Content Strategy

## Content Is Not Filler. Content Is Proof.

Every piece of content WKALAN publishes should either:
- **Teach** something about digital identity
- **Show** what happens when someone is truly understood
- **Share** the philosophy that drives the work

---

## Content Series

### Series 1: Behind Every Website
*Format:* Bilibili video / Xiaohongshu carousel
*Content:* One creator. Their story. How we turned understanding into design.
*Frequency:* 1×/month
*Goal:* Show the process. Humanize the work. Attract similar creators.

### Series 2: 品味人生
*Format:* WeChat article / long-form essay
*Content:* Reflections on design, identity, the creator economy. Philosophical. Personal. Deep.
*Frequency:* 1×/month
*Goal:* Establish WKALAN as a thinking brand, not just a service provider.

### Series 3: Creator Stories
*Format:* Short interview + 3 screenshots
*Content:* Client Q&A. Their words. Their experience.
*Frequency:* After every Pro project (with permission)
*Goal:* Social proof in the client's own voice.

### Series 4: Design Thinking
*Format:* Xiaohongshu carousel
*Content:* "Why we chose this color." "How we found the 3 keywords." "Before → After section breakdown."
*Frequency:* 2×/month
*Goal:* Educate creators about what good digital identity looks like. Make them want it.

---

## Content Rules

### 1. Every piece links back to the portfolio
Content drives traffic. The portfolio converts. Always end with: "See more at wkalan.com/work"

### 2. Client permission for everything
Never post a client's story, face, or work without explicit permission. Signed. Documented.

### 3. Quality over quantity
One great piece per month is better than four mediocre ones. Don't post to fill a calendar. Post because you have something worth sharing.

### 4. Repurpose ruthlessly
One case study → Bilibili video script + Xiaohongshu carousel + WeChat essay + Tweet thread. Create the source material once. Adapt it for every platform.

*Content is not about being seen. It's about being understood — by the right people.*
