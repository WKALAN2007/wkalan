# 006 — Revenue Model

Revenue is not about getting rich. It's about building something sustainable — something that can pay for the tools, the time, and eventually the team.

## Financial Principles

### 1. Profitability from day one
No burn. No runway. No "we'll figure out monetization later." Every project is profitable because costs are near zero.

### 2. Revenue diversity by Year 3
Don't depend on one stream. Services + products + education = resilience.

### 3. Price increases with proof
Every 10 case studies justifies a price increase. The work gets better → the value gets higher → the price follows.

### 4. No debt. No investment (until we choose to).
Bootstrap until the model is proven. Outside money should accelerate something that already works — not fund the search for something that might.
