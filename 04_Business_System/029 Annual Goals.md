## Goal-Setting Rules

### 1. Goals are public (within WKALAN)
Write them down. Track them. Review them quarterly. What gets measured gets done.

### 2. Under-promise, over-deliver
The goals above are floors, not ceilings. Beat them.

### 3. Review every quarter
Q1: Are we on track? Q2: What needs to change? Q3: What's working better than expected? Q4: Plan next year.

### 4. The mission outranks the metrics
If hitting a number requires compromising on depth — the number is wrong. Not the principle.

---

*Goals are not predictions. They're commitments.*
