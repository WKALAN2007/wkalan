# 030 — KPI Dashboard

## What Gets Measured Gets Managed.

---

## Monthly KPIs

| Metric | Target | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| **Inquiries** | 10–15/mo | Top of funnel. Are people finding WKALAN? |
| **Discovery calls booked** | 8–10/mo | Are inquiries converting to conversations? |
| **Projects started** | 4–5/mo | Are conversations converting to commitments? |
| **Projects shipped** | 4–5/mo | Are we delivering? |
| **Avg. project value** | HK$1,000+ | Are clients choosing higher tiers? |
| **Revenue** | HK$4,000+/mo | Is the business healthy? |
| **Client satisfaction** | 4.5/5+ | Are we keeping the promise? |
| **Referral clients** | 1–2/mo | Are clients telling others? |

---

## The Only Metric That Really Matters

All the numbers above are secondary. The primary KPI is:

**"Did someone leave this site feeling like they met a person?"**

Measured by:
- Client feedback after launch
- Audience comments and DMs
- Referral behavior
- Your own honest assessment

If this number is high, everything else follows. If this number drops, no amount of revenue can fix it.

---

## Review Cadence

| Frequency | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| **Weekly** | Check inquiries, projects started, projects shipped |
| **Monthly** | Full KPI review. Adjust targets if needed. |
| **Quarterly** | Deep review. Process improvements. Strategy adjustments. |
| **Annually** | Year review. Next year planning. Major decisions. |

---

*KPIs don't replace judgment. They inform it. The mission always has the final say.*
